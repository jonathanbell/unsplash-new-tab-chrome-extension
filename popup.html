Unsplash New Tab

When opening a new tab you will see random photos from Unsplash.

To see your own photos or your Unsplash likes, please configure the extension by right-clicking the extension's icon and choosing "Options" or click the three dots next to the extension's name in Chrome's extension list.

When opening a new tab you will see @'s on Unsplash.

Unsplash New Tab was made by Jonathan Bell.
