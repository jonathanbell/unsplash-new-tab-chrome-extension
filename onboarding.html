Unsplash New Tab

By default, the extension will show random images from Unsplash when you open a new tab.

In order to show your own Unsplash photos or your liked photos from Unsplash (instead of random images), open the extension's options by clicking the 3 dots next to the extension's name in the Chrome extensions menu. Alternatively, you can pin the extension to the Chrome toolbar and right-click on the extension's icon and choose "Options". Enter your Unsplash username (or the Unsplash username of someone you admire ðŸ˜») and choose the content you want to see.

The first time you open a new tab after installing the extension or changing the extension options, it will take a few moments for a new photo to load. However, subsequent new tabs will load much faster. If the new options don't seem to be immediately effective, open some new tabs. This action will also trigger a cache rebuild, which should then display the newly chosen content on new tabs.
